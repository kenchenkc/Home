Things To Do




An ecozone full of fun things to do.






Boreal Shield

The Boreal Shield is full of fun things to do! In Ontario, there is the Algonquin Park. This park is so big that it is almost as large as the states Delaware & Rhode Island combined. Imagine all of the wildlife and nature just waiting for you to come and visit! At Algonquin Park, you can canoe and fish. If you really want, you can even camp there for a night or two! You can also visit the Gros Morne National park of Newfoundland! There is the infamous site, L'Anse aux Meadows, where the Vikings built the very first European settlement. It is almost like taking a time machine back to the 10th century! But that's not all that Gros Morne National park has to offer. There you can decide to take a dip in the many beaches and coastlines or watch a live theatrical act!

Ottawa

The most visited place in Ottawa is probably the Parliament Hill. In fact, 3 million people visit "The Hill" (common nickname) annually. In The Hill, you can discover the history, functions and art of Canada's Parliament. Another premiere destination in Ottawa is the Byward Market. Here, you can shop, dine, appreciate art, and enjoy the fantastic environment.

St. John's

One of the most unique souvenirs to buy in St. John's is the Jellybean Row. In a nutshell, the Jellybean Row is a series of art prints based on actual homes on the oldest streets of North America. If you're a history buff that loves the railway and water transportation, you must visit the Railway Coastal Museum. Inside, you will find working models of trains, boats, and dioramas that will uncover the story of Newfoundland's railway and coastal boats.
