Soil




Soil that is filled with nutrients, deep down.






Boreal Shield

The soil in the Boreal Shield is heavily leached. This kind of soil occurs where there is a great deal of precipitation. When the water moves downwards, the chemical nutrients is dissolved and gets carried away. Furthermore, this ecozone is full of swampy areas, bare rock, and the topsoil is incredibly thin and poor.
