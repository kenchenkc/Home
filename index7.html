Human Activities




A rich natural resource base.






Boreal Shield

There is a lot to say about the wild range of activities that is done here by humans. Because there is a rich natural resource base in the Boreal Shield, many human activities are centered around that advantage. A few examples are mining, forestry, and trapping.



Ottawa

Ottawa's economy most crucially consists of two major sectors - the federal government and high technology. These two sectors together account for more than 35% of the city's GDP. In fact, Ottawa is the most highly-educated population in the country! Furthermore, Ottawa has the highest amount of engineers, scientists and PhDs per capita in Canada! This highly-educated labour-force has consistently help the country achieve a relatively high median family income.



St. John's

There are quite a few industries in St. John's. One industry is power and electricity generation. Types of resources of power in St. John's is hydro, gas and diesel. Other interesting industries in St. John's include air transportation, petroleum refining, and retail.
