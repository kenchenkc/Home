CYBERBULLYING RESPONSES

Advice

Educate yourself First think Don't respond
To fix the problem, you must understand the problem. So do yourself a favor and fully comprehend what you are dealing with by visiting any website that is about cyberbullying. If anything you post may compromise your reputation, just don’t do it. It’s just more ammo for cyber bullies. If someone bullies you, always remember that your reaction is what the bully wants from you. Your response gives the bully control over the situation. Now who would want that?
Block the bully Google yourself Save the evidence
Block the people or person who is bullying you. If it is in a chat , then leave it. Do constant check ups by searching your name on any major search engine (Google, Bing, etc.) to see if any harmful post about you is made. The one good thing about cyber bullying is that all evidence can be saved. Then the bully can be brought to justice.
